About Us

Task Management System is a simple web application that helps users organize, manage, and track their daily tasks efficiently.

Users can create tasks, update them, monitor progress, view deadlines in the calendar, and improve productivity through an easy-to-use dashboard.

Our goal is to make task management simple, organized, and effective for students and professionals.
